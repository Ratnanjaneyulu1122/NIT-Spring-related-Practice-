	Steps to intragrate validations
	-------------------------------
	1) Add required validator dependency in pom.xml
	2) Add the required validators in model class --> Employee.java
		@NotEmpty, @Email, @Positive, @NotNull, @Size

		<form:errors path="salary"/>
	4)Use @Validated annotation in the controller.
